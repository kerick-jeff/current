# Billing and Invoices

## Where can I find my invoices?

All invoices are available in the Billing section of your account dashboard under Settings > Billing > Invoice History. You can download them as PDFs.

## My card was charged but I don't see the invoice

Invoices are generated within 24 hours of a successful charge. If more than 24 hours have passed and the invoice is not visible, contact support with your transaction ID.

## How do I update my billing information?

Go to Settings > Billing > Payment Methods and click "Update". Changes take effect on the next billing cycle.

## Can I switch from monthly to annual billing?

Yes. Go to Settings > Billing > Plan. You can upgrade to annual billing at any time. The remaining days on your current monthly plan will be prorated and credited.

## Requesting a refund

Refunds are available within 14 days of a charge if you have not exceeded 1,000 API calls during that period. Submit a request via Settings > Billing > Refund Request. Processing takes 5–10 business days.
