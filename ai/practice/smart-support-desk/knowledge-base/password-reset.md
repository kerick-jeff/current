# Password Reset

## How to reset your password

If you cannot log in to your account, you can reset your password using the following steps:

1. Click "Forgot password" on the login page.
2. Enter the email address associated with your account.
3. Check your inbox for a reset link. It expires in 60 minutes.
4. Click the link and choose a new password.

## The reset email is not arriving

- Check your spam folder first.
- Make sure you're checking the same email address used to sign up.
- If it still doesn't arrive after 5 minutes, try requesting a new link.
- Corporate email filters sometimes block automated emails. Try a personal address if possible.

## Locked accounts

After 10 failed login attempts, your account is temporarily locked for 30 minutes as a security measure. This cannot be overridden manually. Wait 30 minutes and try again with the correct credentials.
